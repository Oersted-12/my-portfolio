Algorithm Design

This research project explored an AI-based meal planner designed to generate meal recommendations based on user preferences and constraints. The project focused on applying artificial intelligence concepts to nutrition planning, personalization, and data-driven decision-making.

  • TF-IDF Vectorization: Analyzing ingredient importance across a massive recipe dataset.
  • Cosine Similarity: Calculating mathematical proximity between user preferences and nutritional profiles.
  • Smart Filtering: Real-time dietary constraint handling for personalized safety.

Data Visualization
